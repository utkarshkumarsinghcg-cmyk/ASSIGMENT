SpaceX Launch

SpaceX successfully launched its private space mission from Cape Canaveral, marking another milestone in commercial space exploration. The mission carried satellites for communication, weather monitoring, and scientific research. India’s ISRO also congratulated SpaceX, highlighting the importance of global cooperation in space technology. Analysts predict that private companies will play a larger role in future missions to the Moon and Mars.
