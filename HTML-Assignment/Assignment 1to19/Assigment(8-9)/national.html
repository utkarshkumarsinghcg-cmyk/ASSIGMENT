New Education Policy Announced

The Government of India has rolled out a new education policy that focuses on skill development, digital learning, and multilingual education. Schools across the country will adopt updated curriculums designed to prepare students for modern careers. The policy also emphasizes practical training, coding, and critical thinking from an early stage. Officials say this move will help bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications.

Floods in Assam

Heavy rainfall in Assam has led to severe flooding across multiple districts. Thousands of people have been displaced from their homes, and relief camps have been set up by the state government. Rescue operations are ongoing with the support of the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF). Local authorities have also appealed to citizens for donations of food, medicine, and other essential supplies. Experts warn that climate change is increasing the intensity of seasonal floods in the region.

New Metro Line Opens

Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has inaugurated a new metro line connecting residential areas with business hubs. This expansion aims to reduce traffic congestion and provide faster, eco-friendly travel options to commuters. Citizens welcomed the development, highlighting that the new route will cut down travel time by almost 40 minutes. The government has also announced plans for further metro expansion in upcoming years.
