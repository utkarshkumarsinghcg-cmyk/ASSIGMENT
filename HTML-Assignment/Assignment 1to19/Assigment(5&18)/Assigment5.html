Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a famous South Indian dish made with rice batter and spicy potato filling.

Ingredients:

  • Rice batter
  • Potatoes
  • Spices
    • Mustard
    • Curry leaves
    • Green chilies
  • oil

Cooking Steps:

    Prepare the rice batter.
    Cook the potato filling.
    Heat the pan and spread the batter.
    Add the filling inside.
    Roll and serve hot.

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy curry made with paneer, butter, and tomato gravy.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Spices:
    • Garam masala
    • Chili powder
    • Coriander powder
  • Cream

Cooking Steps:

  • Fry the paneer lightly.
  • Prepare the tomato gravy.
  • Add butter and spices.
  • Mix in paneer cubes.
  • Garnish with cream and coriander.

Cooking Terms:

Simmer
Cooking slowly at low temperature.
Puree
Smooth paste made from blending ingredients.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a sweet dessert made with milk solids, fried, and soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients:

  • Khoya (milk solids)
  • Maida (flour)
  • Sugar syrup:
    • Sugar
    • Water
    • Cardamom
    • Rose water

Cooking Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Dip in sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm.

Cooking Terms:

Knead
Working the dough to develop structure.
Syrup
A thick sweet liquid made from sugar and water.
